Overview

Neurological disorders are conditions that affect your nervous system and how it functions. This module covers the nervous system and how these conditions impact a person's life.

Types of Disorders

There are many types of neurological disorders, including neurodegenerative diseases, epilepsy, brain tumors, and infection-related diseases. This module covers certain types of neurological disorders.

  • Epilepsy
  • Neuromuscular disorders
